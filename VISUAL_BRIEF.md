# Visual Design Brief
## Riri's Kitchen Academy — Character & Environment
**Prepared for Visual Design Team | Confidential | v1.0**

---

## Project Overview

Riri's Kitchen Academy is a children's educational app for Emma (age 4) and Elsie (age 9). Riri is the AI-powered big-sister coach who guides children through cooking-themed learning challenges covering Maths, Spelling, Colours, Counting and more.

This brief covers the visual assets needed to bring Riri and her kitchen world to life. The design should feel like a premium children's game — think Toca Boca quality meets cyberpunk energy. Warm, inviting, safe for kids, but with serious visual style.

---

## Riri — Character Overview

> **Riri is the heart of the app.** She is not a cartoon mascot — she is a fully realised character. Think of her as a cool older sister who happens to love cooking and technology. Kids should want to be her.

### Core Identity

- **Age appearance:** 16–18 years old
- **Ethnicity:** Black British / West African heritage
- **Personality:** Confident, warm, playful, encouraging — never condescending
- **Archetype:** The cool older sibling who makes learning feel like an adventure

### Existing Character Reference

A concept art series has already been created for Riri (attached separately as Riri1.png through Riri5.png). These show her full cyberpunk aesthetic — braids with tech clips, neon shoulder armour, cyberpunk jacket with white panels, glowing cyan shoes, cargo trousers. The existing art is the **canonical reference**. Do not redesign her core look — this brief is about how to adapt her for the kitchen context.

### Character Expression States

The final deliverable must include Riri in the following emotional states, each as a separate illustration:

| State | Description |
|---|---|
| **Idle** | Relaxed, warm smile, gentle stance, weight on one leg |
| **Talking / Coaching** | Animated, leaning forward slightly, hand gesture, mouth open mid-speech |
| **Celebrating** | Arms raised or peace sign (her signature ✌️), big open grin, eyes squinting with joy |
| **Thinking** | Finger to chin or temple, head tilted, contemplative expression, small thought bubbles |
| **Encouraging** | Thumbs up or pointing finger, direct eye contact, warm confident smile |

## The Kitchen — Environment Brief

> **This is the critical brief.** The kitchen must hold both worlds simultaneously: it should be instantly recognisable as a bright, cheerful, child-friendly kitchen AND carry Riri's signature cyberpunk DNA. It is NOT a realistic kitchen. It is NOT a dark sci-fi kitchen. It is a **warm kitchen that has been customised by a tech-savvy teenager.**

### The Concept: "Riri Moved In"

Think about what happens when a cool tech-obsessed teenager moves into a classic bright kitchen and makes it her own. The kitchen structure — tiles, cupboards, window, counter — remains warm and familiar. But Riri has added her own upgrades and personality throughout. The result is a kitchen that feels safe and fun for a 4-year-old, but also stylish enough that a 9-year-old thinks it is cool.

---

### Base Kitchen — Warm & Familiar Elements

These are the foundational elements that must read as a kitchen immediately:

- **Walls:** Pastel checker tiles — peach and white, or soft pink and cream. Warm, not clinical.
- **Window:** Central window with bright sky, sunshine (ideally with a happy face), garden with flowers. Pink or pastel curtains with a brass curtain rod.
- **Cupboards:** Three upper and three lower cabinet units. Cream or off-white with warm brass or gold handles. Classic, chunky, friendly proportions.
- **Counter:** Warm sandy-brown worktop. Full width.
- **Cooker / Hob:** White freestanding cooker with 4 hob rings. Two front rings glowing orange-red when active. Simple, recognisable.
- **Floor:** Classic checkerboard floor tiles in warm cream and biscuit tones.
- **Pendant Lights:** Three simple lampshades hanging from the ceiling — one pink, one blue, one yellow.
- **Counter items:** A fruit bowl (with apple, orange, banana clearly visible), a kettle (blue, simple round shape), a cake tin, a cutting board with chopped vegetables.

---

### Riri's Upgrades — The Cyberpunk Layer

These are the elements Riri has added to the kitchen. Each one should feel like a deliberate personalisation — not random sci-fi decoration, but something a real teenager might actually do to a kitchen they love:

- **Holographic recipe display:** A floating translucent screen or projected recipe card hovering above the counter — showing the current dish being cooked. Neon cyan or purple glow. This replaces a boring cookbook stand.
- **Glowing hob rings:** The active hob rings emit a more vivid, slightly stylised glow — not just orange heat, but with a soft neon pulse that echoes Riri's colour palette.
- **Tech clips on utensil rack:** Hanging utensils (spatulas, ladles, whisks) have small glowing tech clips attached to them — matching the clips in Riri's braids. Subtle Easter egg for fans of the character.
- **Neon LED strip under cabinets:** A subtle cyan or purple LED strip runs under the upper cabinets, providing under-cabinet lighting. One small personalisation that ties the kitchen to her aesthetic.
- **Smart pot / pan:** The red pot on the hob has a subtle glowing ring around the base where it sits on the hob — implying the hob is 'smart'. Not over the top.
- **Her personalised apron:** Riri's apron hangs on a hook near the cooker. It is predominantly black with her signature purple and cyan colour accents. A small triangle logo (her chest emblem) is on the pocket.
- **Spice jars in her colours:** The spice jars on the shelf are in her signature colours — purple, cyan, pink, yellow, green. Label designs have a subtle geometric / tech aesthetic but are still clearly spice jars.
- **Potted plant with glowing pot:** The plant on the windowsill sits in a matte black pot with a subtle glowing cyan ring around the base. Simple, clean.

---

### What to AVOID

> If a child would not recognise it as a kitchen immediately, it has gone too far. The cyberpunk elements should feel like **decoration and personalisation** — not structural. The warmth and colour of the kitchen must always win.

- ❌ Dark backgrounds or near-black walls
- ❌ Dramatic neon-soaked scenes where the kitchen glow drowns out the pastels
- ❌ Chrome or metallic surfaces replacing the warm wood/cream cabinet aesthetic
- ❌ Floating UI elements that obscure the kitchen background
- ❌ Visual complexity that would overwhelm a 4-year-old

## Colour Palette

The palette must work in both directions — warm kitchen pastels and Riri's neon signature colours must coexist without clashing. **The kitchen pastels are dominant. Riri's neon colours are accent.**

### Riri's Signature Colours (accent use only)

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Riri Purple | `#9B30FF` | Primary accent — tech clips, LED strips, glowing rings |
| Riri Cyan | `#00D4FF` | Secondary accent — shoe glow, holographic elements |
| Riri Pink | `#FF4FCE` | Tertiary accent — shoulder armour detail, sparkles |
| Riri Orange | `#FF7A00` | Warm neon — used sparingly on hot elements |

### Kitchen Base Palette (dominant)

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Kitchen Peach | `#FFE8D6` | Wall tiles (alternating) |
| Kitchen Cream | `#FFF8F0` | Wall tiles (alternating), cabinet faces |
| Warm Wood | `#F0B870` | Counter top, shelf boards |
| Warm White | `#FFFCF4` | Cabinet doors, cooker body |
| Sandy Floor A | `#F0E6D0` | Floor tile (alternating) |
| Sandy Floor B | `#E4D8C0` | Floor tile (alternating) |

> **Rule:** When Riri's neon colours appear in the kitchen context, they should always be used as accents on small elements (clips, LED strips, glowing rings) — never as dominant fills or backgrounds.

## Typography Notes

Typography is handled in-app and does not need to be designed as part of this brief. For reference, the app uses:

- **Display / headings:** Boogaloo (Google Fonts) — rounded, friendly, bold
- **Body / UI:** Nunito — warm, rounded, highly legible for children

If any in-scene text is needed (e.g. labels on the holographic recipe screen, spice jar labels), it should match this rounded, friendly aesthetic while incorporating Riri's geometric/tech styling.

## Deliverables Required

### 1. Riri Character Sprites *(Priority 1)*

| Asset | Specification | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Idle | Full body, standing, transparent background | PNG, 1000×1800px min |
| Talking / Coaching | Full body, gesture pose, transparent background | PNG, 1000×1800px min |
| Celebrating | Full body, arms raised or peace sign, transparent background | PNG, 1000×1800px min |
| Thinking | Full body, contemplative pose, transparent background | PNG, 1000×1800px min |
| Encouraging | Full body, direct/pointing gesture, transparent background | PNG, 1000×1800px min |

### 2. Kitchen Background Scene *(Priority 1)*

- Full kitchen background illustration as described in the environment brief above
- Format: PNG or layered PSD/AI — **1920×1080px** minimum (landscape). Also provide portrait crop **1080×1920px** for mobile.
- Must work as a static background — Riri character sprites are overlaid separately in-app
- Provide two versions: **(a)** daytime / bright, **(b)** optional evening version with warmer lighting

### 3. Riri Avatar / Headshot *(Priority 1)*

- Close crop of Riri's face and shoulders — used as a small circular avatar chip in the UI
- Format: PNG, **400×400px**, circular crop or with transparent background for circular masking
- Should match the Talking/Coaching expression — engaged and warm

### 4. Dish Card Illustrations *(Priority 2)*

Four illustrated dish card backgrounds — one per dish in the app. Each should feel like a miniature illustrated kitchen scene in Riri's style:

| Dish | Scene Colour Direction |
|---|---|
| Chocolate Cake | Purple/pink gradient with cake elements |
| Yummy Pizza | Warm orange/red with pizza elements |
| Rainbow Soup | Teal/blue with soup pot elements |
| Super Smoothie | Green with blender and fruit elements |

Format: PNG, **600×700px** each, used as card backgrounds in the menu.

## Technical Specifications

| Spec | Detail |
|---|---|
| Primary Platform | iPad / tablet browser (Safari). Secondary: desktop Chrome. |
| Screen Sizes | Design at 1080×1920px portrait. Must scale to 768×1024px (iPad). |
| Riri Sprite Scale | Full-body Riri occupies approx 60–70% of screen height when standing centre-frame. |
| Background Layout | Kitchen bg fills full screen. Counter sits at approx 60% down the screen. |
| File Formats | PNG with transparency for all character/overlay assets. PNG or WebP for backgrounds. |
| Colour Mode | sRGB. All final exports at 72dpi minimum (screen only, no print). |
| Animation | Static illustrations only at this stage. Animations are handled programmatically in-app. Poses must work as still images. |

## Tone & Style References

> These are reference points to align visual language — not sources to copy from.

### Aim for this energy

- **Toca Boca / Toca Kitchen** — chunky, bold outlines, bright flat colour, immediately readable shapes, age-appropriate joy
- **Peppa Pig World games** — warm kitchen environments, pastel tiles, recognisable objects at a glance
- **Subway Surfers** — high-fidelity character illustration with expressive animation poses, vibrant colour palette, premium game feel
- **Steven Universe** — representation, warmth, character depth in a cartoon format. Rich skin tones, natural hair celebrated
- **Afrofuturism aesthetic** — Riri's tech elements should feel culturally rooted. Think Wakanda tech sensibility, not generic sci-fi

### Steer away from

- **Generic anime styles** — Riri is not anime. She is distinctly Black British.
- **Western superhero comic style** — too stiff, too muscular, wrong energy for a 4-year-old's coach
- **Over-rendered photorealism** — this is a children's app. Stylisation and warmth over realism.
- **Generic AI-generated character aesthetics** — Riri has a defined identity. Do not let the output look AI-generated.
